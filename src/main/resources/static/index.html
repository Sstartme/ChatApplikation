How can I make my henna design last longer?

To make your henna design last longer, keep it dry for at least 6-12 hours after application, avoid frequent washing, and apply a natural oil (like olive or coconut oil) to the design to enhance its longevity. Enhance the richness of your henna by applying a mixture of lemon juice and sugar. This combination not only deepens the henna's color but also brings out its natural beauty.

How can I remove henna stains from the skin?

Henna stains will naturally fade over time.

Can henna be applied to areas other than hands and feet?

Henna can be applied to various body parts, including arms, legs, back, and even the belly. The choice of placement depends on personal preferences and the design's complexity.
